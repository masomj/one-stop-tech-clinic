PS4 Repairs

Does your PS4 sound like a jet engine? Does it show you the overheating screen? Thats likely caused by a build of dust over time in the system, completely clogging up the cooling system. This kind of repair is something we do regularly here, and is likely our most popular job. We're experts at sorting this issue.

Even if your issue isnt related to overheating, we can still help. Maybe your PS4 randomly turns off, or do games just disappear. Perhaps you can only get it to start in safe mode. Just speak to us via facebook, or our email (onestoptechlcinic@gmail.com) and we'll get it sorted.

Computer Repairs

We dont just build PCs, we troubleshoot them to. If your desktop pc is running slow, crashing a lot or has any other issues, All it takes is One Stop with us to get it back up and running.

Some of our previous fixes include an aging system from the windows Vista days. The systems power supply had degraded and could no longer power all of the expansion cards in the system. As the system was an HP, it used a proprietary motherboard power connector, so the fix was to remove a wireless card and use a USB Wi-Fi receiver instead.

If you have any issues with your PC, whether it be software or hardware oriented, contact us today to see what we can do for you.
